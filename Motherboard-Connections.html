Motherboard connections

The motherboard connections is how the motherboard connects to all the compnents of the system that are not
directly connected busses on the motherboard. These connections include:

  • SATA connections: This is how the motherboard connects to the HDDs and SDDs and the disk drives, if there
    any.
  • 3 or 4-pin connections: These are how the fans will connect to the motherboard.
  • USB headers: This is where the USB slots on the front of the tower will connect to the motherboard.
  • Misc. Connectors: This is where the rest of the connections coming from the front panel of the tower
    will connect to the motherboard, which include any buttons that turn the system on/off or reset it
    and the audio input/output jacks.
